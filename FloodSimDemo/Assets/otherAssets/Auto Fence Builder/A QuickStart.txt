Auto Fence Builder 1.0.1

Load Demo Scene ( or create your a new one with either a terrain or a plane to act as the ground)

Add Auto Fence Builder from the Game Object menu, or press shift-alt-F.

Shift-click 2 or 3 times in the scene view (in editor mode). You should now see your fence.

Select any style of fence from 'Choose Preset' popupmenu.

If you have placed the fence on uneven ground, and use a Panel rail, switch 'Slope Mode' to 'Sheared'

You can now adjust any of the parameters of the fence, such as 'Fence Height'

For better viual quality, enable Anti-Aliasing in the Edit Menu/Project Settings/Quality

See the manual for more details.
